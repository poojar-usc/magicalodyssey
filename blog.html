Magical Odyssey

Production Plan



  • Finals

    End of our Journey!

    In the final week, we were documenting everything we built in these past 15 weeks. We also recorded a basic gameplay video illustrating how the game works. We finally concluded our week with a live demo explaining how the game was fabricated.

  • Week 14

    Integration & Beautification

    Since all the levels and segues were built separately, now it's time for integrating all those subparts to a complete game structure. Along with this,we also started adding SFX and VFX to our levels to enhance the UI of the overall game.

  • Week 12

    Game Completion

    We finally completed our game's basic layout and structure for each levels. It can perform all the necessary functions that are required in each level.

  • Week 10

    Completed Level 3

    At this stage, we were done with 70% of our game. We finally made a list of spells we will be using for the game in the last level.

  • Midterms

    Halfway through our game!

    By this time, we were completely done with Levels 1 and 2. We had also started working on the basic layout for Level 3.

  • Week 7

    Still working on Level 2

    Since it was our first major level, we took our sweet time building this one!

  • Week 5

    Achieved our first milestone

    We finalized our Level 1 by this week. Along with this, we also started preparing for our next milestone, Level 2. We began searching for Level 2 assets and also began fabricating our own prefabs from this point.

  • Week 3

    Magical Odyssey Development Begins!

    After deciding a basic layout of what the game looks like; what levels it will have, etc, we moved on to start building Level 1 of our game. Collected relevant assets. Set up an environment.

  • Week 1

    Brainstorming Ideas

    We formed our incredible team and then started discussing about what game we should build. Along with that, we also started familiarizing ourselves with Unity Hub.
