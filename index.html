Magical Odyssey
Magical Odyssey is a single-player, Adventure, Fantasy, 3D-RPG Game available across all Android devices. The game revolves around the life of our infamous young wizard, Harry Potter and his adventures through Hogwarts School of Witchcraft & Wizardry. The wizard encounters some harsh run-ins with another wizard, Pansy Parkinson, and is quickly left to fend for himself. The wizard gains experience with each progressing level and finally gets to fight his rival in a spell battle. The game would work as a learning experience for players who want to gain more knowledge about spells and wizardry.

Level 1

Starting School

Along with an acceptance letter from Hogwarts, Harry also gets a list of items that he would need in school. So, in the beginning of this level, he runs around, collecting all the items that he would need to start school. The latter part of a level involves an interactive quiz that sorts the player into a house based on the choices from his answers to the quiz.

Level 2

Quidditch Match

Harry is challenged to a game of Quidditch to prove his worth to his newly sorted house. As a part of the quidditch game, the player needs to shoot the quaffle through any of the three hoops and score a minimum of 1000 points within a fixed time of 60 seconds. The hoops are blocked by three keeper’s preventing the player from scoring.

Level 3

Learning Advanced Spells

After the quidditch match, the player must boost his skills to beat his rival in Level 4. Harry has to find his way through a maze in search of the spell orbs that he has to collect in order to learn and cast those spells in future. While finding and learning those spells, he encounters various obstacles like Aragog (the spider), Peeves (the poltergeist), and a dementor in the maze that he has to defeat using the learnt spells.

Level 4

The Duelling Battle

The player, till now, has successfully picked up supplies for school, got sorted into a house, participated and won the Quidditch Match, and learnt advanced spells like Stupefy, Reducto, Expelliarmus, etc. In the final level, he has to face Pansy Parkinson from the enemy house and hopefully convince her into joining Dumbledore’s Army, irrespective of the win or loss.
